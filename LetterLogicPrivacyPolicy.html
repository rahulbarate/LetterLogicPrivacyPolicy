Privacy Policy for Letter Logic

Effective Date: 6 February 2026

Letter Logic does not collect any personally identifiable information from users directly. However, the game uses third-party services that may collect certain information for analytics, advertising, and core game-related functionality.

Third-party services used:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying banner and rewarded advertisements
  • Unity Technologies – for the core game engine and related services

These third-party services may collect information such as:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system, and advertising ID)
  • Crash logs and basic performance or diagnostic data

Third-party privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Unity Privacy Policy

Children’s Privacy:

Letter Logic does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through third-party services used in the game, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.
